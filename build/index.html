Your Key Responsibilities

Coordinate Travel Arrangements:
Plan and book all travel-related needs including flights, hotel accommodation, and ground transportation.

Manage Personal Scheduling and Reminders:

Keep track of important personal dates and events such as medical appointments, birthdays, and anniversaries. Set reminders and ensure timely preparation or notifications.
